⚠️ Chemical Safety Quick Reference (SDS)

Tap any card for full details • For emergency use only

🔴 SUPER D 500 / PERSYNT 500

50% Hydrogen Peroxide (H₂O₂)

⚠️ Strong Oxidizer (Cat 2) • Corrosive • Fire/Explosion risk

PPE: Butyl/neoprene gloves, face shield, goggles, flame-resistant clothing

  • Can cause fire or explosion on contact with combustibles
  • Severe burns to skin and eyes
  • Keep away from heat, sparks, organics

🟠 VIGOROX WWT II

Evonik • PAA 15% + H₂O₂ 24%

⚠️ Corrosive • Combustible • Severe burns

PPE: Butyl/neoprene gloves, goggles + face shield, protective clothing

  • Biocide — used in wastewater treatment
  • Very toxic to aquatic life
  • Never mix with other chemicals

🟡 SPECTRUM 22

Evonik • PAA 20-23% + H₂O₂ 8.5-10.5%

⚠️ Corrosive • Oxidizer • Severe burns

PPE: Butyl gloves, face shield + goggles, apron, respirator if vapors

  • Used for poultry processing
  • Causes severe skin/eye burns
  • May cause respiratory irritation

🚛 DOT PLACARDS & ERG HANDBOOK

Two Required Placards

Class 5.1 or 5.2 Oxidizer (yellow) + Class 8 Corrosive (white/black)

PPE: Butyl gloves, face shield + goggles, apron, respirator if vapors

  • ERG Guide 140 – Oxidizers (2024)
  • Initial isolation: 150–300 ft
  • Call CHEMTREC 1-800-424-9300
